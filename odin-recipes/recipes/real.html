Real Sopapillas

Description

Make real Sopapillas by combining flour, baking powder, salt, and shortening. Knead the dough, roll it out, and cut it into squares or triangles. Fry until golden brown and puffy. Serve warm with powdered sugar and honey.

Create authentic Sopapillas at home for a taste of Mexico. These crispy, fluffy pastries are a delight. Share them with loved ones and enjoy the irresistible combination of golden dough, cinnamon, sugar, and sweet syrup.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Steps:

  1. Combine flour, baking powder, salt, and shortening in a bowl, and mix until crumbly.
  2. Gradually add warm water to the mixture, kneading until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Let the dough rest for a short while, then roll it out into a thin sheet.
  4. Cut the dough into small squares or triangles using a knife or a pastry cutter.
  5. Fry the Sopapillas in hot oil until they turn golden brown and puffy, then remove and drain on a paper towel. Serve warm, dusted with powdered sugar and accompanied by honey or a sweet syrup.
