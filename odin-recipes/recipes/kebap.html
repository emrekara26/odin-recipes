Beef Shish Kebap

Description

Marinate, skewer, and grill tender meat with flavorful spices and veggies. Serve with flatbread, tzatziki sauce, and a salad. Savor the smoky, grilled goodness!

Experience the delight of shish kebab: marinated meat, vibrant vegetables. Skewer, grill, and enjoy with flatbread, tzatziki sauce. A Mediterranean favorite for smoky, savory satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Choose your preferred meat
  • Marinade: Prepare a marinade using olive oil
  • Vegetables: Select colorful vegetables like bell peppers
  • Skewers: Use metal or wooden skewers
  • Garnish: Optional garnishes can include freshly chopped parsley

Steps:

  1. Prepare the marinade by combining olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, salt, pepper, and your choice of herbs and spices.
  2. Marinate the meat by coating it with the prepared marinade and letting it sit in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or overnight for better flavor infusion.
  3. Thread the marinated meat onto skewers, alternating with colorful vegetables like bell peppers, onions, and cherry tomatoes.
  4. Grill the skewers over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked to your desired level of doneness and the vegetables are slightly charred and tender.
  5. Serve the şiş kebap hot, garnished with fresh herbs, accompanied by flatbread, rice, salad, and your favorite sauces or condiments.
